Books that I recommend

This is the second book of the author Holly Black's trilogy called the Folk of the Air. it is my most favorite book in the trilogy with its intense drama, romance, and fantasy. It is also about world building where the story is different from the other books with a different lore that relates to faeries and humans. If you love reading romance and fantasy, this book is for you.

This book is the first book of the series "Off Campus" by Elle Kennedy. It is one of my favorite sport romance as it explores hockey and the relationship of the two leading couple as they form their friendship, trust, and love. It is on a college setting with the couple leading a different life from each other.

Another book from a trilogy I would to recommend is by Lauren Roberts, Fearless. It is a dystopian and fantasy book about a girl rising from the ground up and earning her place. This is a fantasy, drama, and romance genre with a slight hint of pain and angst.
